  • Course outline: We will try to cover most of Part 1 (Chapters 1 - 11) and, time permitting, some topics in Part 5 (Chapters 24, 27, 29 ...). Here is a brief outline:
    • Groups: definition and examples: symmetries of plane figures, cyclic groups, permutation groups, finite groups etc.
    • Subgroups, examples; group isomorphisms and homomorphisms.
    • Cosets and Lagrange's theorem.
    • Normal subgroups, quotients.
    • Fundamental theorem of finite abelian groups.
    • Sylow theorems.
    • Symmetry groups, group actions.
